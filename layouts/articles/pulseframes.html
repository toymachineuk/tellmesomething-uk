Focusing on Storytelling

How We Came Up with the Idea

PulseFrames didn't begin as a product—it began with a simple question. Why should great stories disappear after 24 hours, while traditional websites often struggle to capture attention?

We set out to combine the best of both worlds: the permanence and discoverability of the open web with the immersive, engaging experience of modern storytelling.

Developed through international collaboration and refined within Finland's innovation-driven startup ecosystem, PulseFrames continues to evolve as a new way to tell stories on the open web.
Three key differences

Why Are PulseFrame Stories More Beneficial Than Instagram or Facebook Stories?

Every Story takes time to create, yet on most social platforms it disappears after just 24 hours. Once it's gone, so is its ability to attract new visitors or generate long-term value.

PulseFrame Stories are built differently. They remain online for as long as you want, can be discovered through search engines, and belong to you—not the platform.

Here's how PulseFrame compares with Instagram and Facebook Stories. No platform lock-in. Your story. Your rules.

Once published, a PulseFrame Story becomes a searchable asset that continues attracting visitors and supporting your marketing.

With PulseFrame, the story creator is the sole owner of the story. Keep a copy on your own computer or publish it anywhere on the open web—it's entirely your choice.

There's no need to reach follower milestones before social networks allow you to include links in your Stories. Every PulseFrame Story can include links from day one, along with many other features that simply aren't available in social media Stories.

Where PulseFrames Can Be Used

Six Ways PulseFrames Create Impact

Discover six ways interactive storytelling delivers more engaging, memorable, and effective experiences than traditional web pages.

Product Launches

Go beyond a standard landing page. Showcase features, explain how your product works, answer common questions, and demonstrate its value through an interactive experience visitors can explore at their own pace.

Brand Storytelling

Tell the story behind your brand—from where you began to where you're headed. Bring together your mission, milestones, products, values, and achievements in an engaging format that helps people connect with your business.

Event Promotion

Build anticipation before your event and keep attendees informed throughout. Combine schedules, speakers, venue details, maps, videos, updates, and highlights into one interactive destination.

Case Studies & Professional Portfolios

Showcase your expertise with visual, interactive case studies. Whether you're a consultant, architect, designer, lawyer, doctor, or agency, demonstrate your experience, process, and results in a compelling way.

Social Media Archives

Your best content deserves more than a fleeting moment in the feed. Turn important posts into organised, searchable stories that stay discoverable, shareable, and valuable long after they're published.

Travel Guides

Create immersive destination guides that combine maps, recommendations, accommodation, itineraries, videos, contact details, and local insights into a seamless interactive experience.
