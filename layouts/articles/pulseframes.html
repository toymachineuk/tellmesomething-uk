Most content disappears in a social feed within hours.
PulseFrames are interactive stories that live on the open web, giving your content a permanent home that continues to attract visitors, rank in search engines, and drive traffic back to your brand.

Think of them as lightweight microsites designed for a single story, campaign, product, or event. Rich with images, video, audio, maps, and interactive elements, every PulseFrame is built to capture attention, encourage sharing, and keep audiences engaged.

Instead of publishing content that fades away, create experiences that keep working long after they're launched.

CHAPTER I

How It Started

PulseFrames didn't begin as a product—it began as a question.

Websites offered permanence but little engagement. Social media captured attention, yet valuable content disappeared almost as quickly as it was published.

We set out to combine the best of both worlds: the permanence of the open web with the immersive experience of modern storytelling.

Developed through international collaboration and refined within Finland's innovation-driven startup ecosystem.

Why it Matters?

Social media stories are designed to keep people inside someone else's platform. They're excellent for reaching audiences in the moment—but their impact is often short-lived. PulseFrames are built for the open web. They become permanent, searchable assets that continue attracting visitors, supporting your marketing, and telling your story long after they're published.
