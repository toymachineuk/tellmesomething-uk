Focusing on Storytelling

How We Came Up with the Idea

PulseFrames didn't begin as a product—it began with a simple question. Why should great stories disappear after 24 hours, while traditional websites often struggle to capture attention?

We set out to combine the best of both worlds: the permanence and discoverability of the open web with the immersive, engaging experience of modern storytelling.

Developed through international collaboration and refined within Finland's innovation-driven startup ecosystem, PulseFrames continues to evolve as a new way to tell stories on the open web.

Three key differences

Why Are PulseFrame Stories More Beneficial Than Instagram or Facebook Stories?

If you've ever created Stories for social media, you know how much effort goes into keeping your audience engaged. It's a shame to see all that work disappear after just 24 hours, as if it never existed.
PulseFrame Stories are different. They're permanent. The owner of the story is the only one who decides how long it will live.

Read on to discover the key differences PulseFrame offers content creators. No platform lock-in. Your story. Your rules.

Once published, a PulseFrame Story becomes a searchable asset that continues attracting visitors and supporting your marketing.

With PulseFrame, the story creator is the sole owner of the story. Keep a copy on your own computer or publish it anywhere on the open web—it's entirely your choice.

There's no need to reach follower milestones before social networks allow you to include links in your Stories. Every PulseFrame Story can include links from day one, along with many other features that simply aren't available in social media Stories.

Process

How to Create Your PulseFrame Story

Unlike social stories that disappear, a PulseFrame becomes a permanent, searchable destination that continues bringing people back to your brand.

01

Share Your Vision

Every great story starts with an idea. Share yours in whatever way feels most natural. You can use our storyboard template to outline each slide, or simply tell us your vision and we'll create the narrative for you.

02

Refine Together

We believe the best ideas take time. Every project includes three rounds of revisions, giving you the opportunity to review, reflect, and refine your story until it feels just right.

03

Own It

Your PulseFrame story is your digital asset. Once it's complete, we'll provide you with a copy, giving you full ownership and the freedom to publish it whenever you're ready.

04

Publish

When it's time to go live, you can publish your story on your own website or landing page. If you don't have one, we'll host it on our domain. Unlike social media posts, you'll always own your story and keep a permanent copy.

05

Share Everywhere

One story. Unlimited reach. Share your PulseFrame story through social media, email, WhatsApp, SMS, presentations, digital business cards, or QR codes—wherever your audience is..

05

Grow Your Business

PulseFrame story keeps working long after it's published. Use it to showcase your achievements, build credibility, answer questions, and create new opportunities with every visitor.
